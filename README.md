# Budgetify
A simple SQLite Application for a partner/ roommate.<br>
Facing problems with friends, roommate, partner in terms of money? Now, make your life easier. <br>
Maintain a budget. This app keeps a list of all items and cost you spent on. Now, just spend and keep a note. <br>
